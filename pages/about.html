About Us

Homely food is the local food service business in Melbourne. Homely Food serves meals that are home-cooked from our passionate chefs.
We aim to provide a great diversity of food from different cultures and bringing our authentic cultural cuisines right to your doorsteps.

Just like traveling the world and experiencing local cuisines, Homely Food brings to you a weekly menu where you can pre-order
and experience our many authentic cuisines without having to travel around.
